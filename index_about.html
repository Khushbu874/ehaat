About E-Haat

E-Haat project is an online platform dedicated to empowering artisans and small business owners by providing them a digital marketplace to showcase and sell thier handmade products and crafts .

About E-Haat Logo

First of all, this is the logo of our platform. It is shown that Chhattisgarh is called the bowl of rice and there are balls of rice on both sides. And after that, a picture of folk art of Bastar in the middle. Now the name of our platform is E Haat. E means electronic and Haat means market. In Chhattisgarh the market is called Haat. That's why we have named our platform E-Haat.
